I created a Material Gatepass System that digitizes the manual process of tracking materials entering and leaving the company. Instead of relying on printed templates and handwritten records, materials are now selected and endorsed digitally for approval and signature. Once confirmed, the gatepass can be exported as a PDF, printed for guard verification, then scanned back into the system for permanent record storage. This ensures faster processing, secure documentation, and easier audits since all records are centralized and accessible even if hard copies are discarded.”
